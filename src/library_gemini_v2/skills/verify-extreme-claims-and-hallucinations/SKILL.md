# Verify Extreme Claims and Hallucinations

1. **Beware the 'Blank Screen' Fallacy**: Dark images or dark-themed edits (e.g., 'dark, aged metal') are frequently and incorrectly hallucinated as 'blank black screens'. Always look closely at dark images to identify textures, shapes, and content before claiming they are empty.
2. **Check for Contradictions**: Ensure your reasoning does not contradict the prompt. If the prompt asks for a 'nipple', do not state that the image 'introduces a nipple, which is not part of the instruction'.
3. **Verify Before Penalizing**: If you are about to give a score of 1 for 'completely failing' or 'destroying the image', double-check if you are simply misinterpreting a dark or low-contrast image.
