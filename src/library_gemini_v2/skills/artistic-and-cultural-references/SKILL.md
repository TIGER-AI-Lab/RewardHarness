# Artistic and Cultural References

When a prompt references a specific artist (e.g., 'Claes Oldenburg'), art style, or cultural reference, you MUST evaluate based on the hallmarks of that reference.

1. **Define the Reference:** First, identify what the reference means. For example, Claes Oldenburg is famous for massive, oversized, often soft or drooping sculptures of everyday objects.
2. **Expect Stylization:** An image that successfully mimics a surreal or abstract style will inherently look 'unrealistic' or 'distorted' compared to a normal photo.
3. **Do Not Penalize for Lack of Realism:** DO NOT penalize images for lacking realism or looking 'artificial' when the requested style is inherently stylized. Reward the image that best captures the essence of the requested artist or style.
