---
description: Guidelines for evaluating full setting transformations and ensuring exact
  thematic matches.
name: evaluating-thematic-and-setting-accuracy
type: skill
---

When evaluating full setting transformations (e.g., 'elementary school cafeteria', 'sold out concert', 'underground New York subway'), look for specific distinguishing elements. A classroom with desks is NOT a cafeteria. A room with a few people is NOT a sold-out concert. A generic train station is NOT a New York subway. If the edited image retains too much of the original setting (e.g., keeping a restaurant table when asked for a school cafeteria), penalize its instruction score.
