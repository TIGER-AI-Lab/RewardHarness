---
description: Guidelines for evaluating edits that change the atmosphere, setting,
  or context of a scene.
name: scene-and-atmosphere-transformation
type: skill
---

When evaluating prompts that change the scene's context (e.g., 'sold out concert', 'elementary school cafeteria'):
1. **Contextual Elements**: Check for the addition of specific elements that define the new scene.
   - *Sold out concert*: Large dense crowds, stage lighting, atmospheric effects (fog/lasers), raised hands.
   - *Elementary school cafeteria*: Institutional tables, lunch trays, children, cafeteria background.
2. **Integration and Realism**: The added elements must blend naturally with the original subjects. Check for consistent lighting, appropriate scale, and realistic shadows.
3. **Preservation of Main Subject**: The original main subject should remain the focal point and retain their identity, even if the environment around them changes drastically.
