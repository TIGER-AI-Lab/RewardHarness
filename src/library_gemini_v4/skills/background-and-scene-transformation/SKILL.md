# Background and Scene Transformation

When evaluating prompts that change the setting (e.g., "Make it seem like a sold out concert", "Change background to a grassy savannah"):

1. **Background Accuracy**: Does the new background accurately reflect the prompt? (e.g., a sold-out concert needs a large crowd and stage lighting, not just a dark room).
2. **Subject Integration**: The original foreground subject must blend naturally into the new scene.
   - **Lighting & Shadows**: Does the lighting on the subject match the new background? (e.g., dramatic lighting for a concert, bright sunlight for a savannah).
   - **Edges**: Are there visible artifacts, halos, or jagged cutouts around the subject?
   - **Perspective**: Does the subject's placement make sense in the new environment?
3. **Subject Preservation**: The identity, pose, and key details of the main subject should remain unchanged unless the prompt implies otherwise.
