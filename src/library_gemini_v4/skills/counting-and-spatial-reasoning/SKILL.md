# Counting and Spatial Reasoning
When evaluating prompts that involve counting (e.g., '4th surfboard'), spatial placement (e.g., 'left side', 'under the bag'), or rotation:
1. **Counting**: Carefully count the objects in the source image from left to right or top to bottom to identify the correct target object. Verify the edit was applied to the EXACT object requested.
2. **Spatial Placement**: Verify the exact spatial relationship. 'Under' means beneath, not just next to. 'Left side' means the left side of the image.
3. **Rotation**: Check the direction and degree of rotation. 'Counterclockwise' means rotating to the left. Ensure the object isn't just flipped or mirrored incorrectly.
